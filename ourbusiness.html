OUR BUSINESS

What is SPrMA?

We offer estate management services to residential estates, predominantly in the Centurion area. The company is a registered business and the owner holds various qualifications applicable and relevant for the management of an estate. Read more about this on our ABOUT US page.

What is an estate manger? One who works with property owners to manage and oversee the estate. An estate manger has to have excellent communication and management skills, be a quick problem-solver who can work independently with little direction. Good organisation skills is also important, as they often negotiate with contractors/service providers on various projects and set up financial budgets for the estate.

SPrMA is a well-structured business, where you are dealing with a company, not an individual. We take on the HR and personnel management functions, financial and legal functions, so you don’t have to employ dozens of staff, making it cost effective. The business has an estate management office, so there is always someone available that you can call for assistance – including over vacation periods. You also have access to a list of proven, vetted and preferred suppliers.

What we do, inter alia:

Strategic

  • Evaluate and improve entry/exit structures and protocols.
  • Perimeter status – evaluate. Manage upgrades.
  • Evaluate and recommend updates to the budget with focus on increasing revenue and optimising expenses.
  • Evaluate and recommend updates to the House Rules.
  • Evaluate status of revenue collections. Liaise with Managing Agent and Attorneys to improve the current situation. Broker agreements with defaulters.
  • Maintain a Risk Register, recommend mitigation actions and implement.

Contact and knowledge

  • Maintain contact with Managing Agent. Assist residents with queries.
  • Prepare letters as required to residents, developers and other stakeholders. Send via Managing Agent or directly.
  • Maintain contact with City of Tshwane and Area Councillors. Establish strong relationships with objective of getting onsite issues resolved quickly.
  • Maintain contact with and administer onsite security activities.

Onsite availability

  • As required by client.
  • Present for meetings after hours if required with residents, Directors, AGM, SGM, Security.

Onsite tasks

  • Monitor condition and supervise cleaning of Swimming Pool, park facilities, clubhouse and office.
  • Monitor condition and supervise cleaning of streets including weeds, rubble and tar.
  • Supervise and assist Security with the entry and exit of contractors as per an approved list, intercom system and entrance and exit gate motors.
  • Assist with identification and secure entry of new owners and tenants. Biometric registration.
  • Assist Architect with final inspection in accordance with the Architectural Design Guidelines (ADG). Gates, washing lines must be installed and lawn must be planted, rubble must be removed.

  • Clubhouse bookings.

  • Assist with any security incidents and resolution thereof.
  • On instruction from the Directors, clean stands where Owners have failed to clean after providing warning.
  • Monitor Residents day to day compliance to House Rules.
  • Maintain Asset Register.

Garden Service

  • Clubhouse area, outside Guardhouse, spraying for ants and weeds, trees trimming and plants watering.
  • Paving clean and spray, car park area included.
  • Draw up roster for General Assistants. monitor and control.

Procurement and Sales

  • Develop and implement a procurement policy.
  • Purchase items from suppliers as required.
  • Maintain the petty cash account.
  • Administer Clubhouse rental.
  • Administer/supervise HOA Contractors.
  • Draw up Subcontractors or SLA as required.

General

  • Equipment and Tools – manage storage, neatness and cleanliness.
  • Dolomite Risk Management Plan.

Minutes and resolutions

  • Minutes of all meetings with Managing Agent to be taken and distributed.
